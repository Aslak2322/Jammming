About Me

"Career"

Hello my name is Aslak Jakob Bennedsen and i am 23 years old. I grew up in copenhagen, and i have lived there for most of my life, except for 2.5 years, where i lived in france, when i was 8. I went to a public school called oesterfarimagsgade skole, followed by high school at christianshavns gymnasium.

During my second year in high school i had a job at a firm called flyhjaelp. There we helped customers whose flight was delayed, get compensation from airline. Following high school i worked as a phone salesman for four months. Just after I quit as a phone salesman, i broke my ankle skateboarding. It was a complicated injury, followed by an infection in the foot resulting in a total of 5 surgeries and 7 months on crutches. The 6th month on crutches i got a job at a firm called insurello, there i helped insurance customers get money from their insurances. Then i had a job as a bartender for 3 months. Now i am working at a call center in greece.

During my time at home on crutches, i started learning, and i started learning a lot. Prior to the injury i had started reading non-fiction books, and started playing chess. Now was the perfect time to pick up the pace.
